Problem

One goal of Red Nose Day is that every child understands why they are fundraising. Teachers struggled to cover the issues of behind Red Nose Day in lessons time, as they lacked flexible, curriculum-linked resources.

What I did

I led project team to create an interactive story that teachers could use with pupils as part of ICT lessons.

Prototyping content and story flow with children and teachers helped design the right language level and interactivity of the story. I also developed promotional emails and supporting content across the web user journey.

The story included a follow up quiz to gauge comprehension levels at the end of the quiz.
