Problem

Introducing users who were familiar with a legacy system to a new process.

What I did

Border Force officers had to write reports were familiar with an old system. The technology dictated the process rather than helping users do the jobs that need to be done.

By observing how users wrote report and their pain points with the legacy system, I was able to write microcopy, onboarding and help content to help users adapt to the new report writing flow.

Result

The service replaced the legacy report writing system and has a 90% high satisfaction rating from users.
